About
Our Mission

To allow banks and other financial providers to detect fraudulent transactions. Today, purchases through credit and debit cards is the main way of making transactions for the majority of people. As we are moving towards a digital world, cyber-security is becoming a crucial part of our life.



How to Use
Users will have the option to start a scan which will identify and list all transactions that have been marked as fraudulent. Users can also utilise the 'notebook' and 'observations' feature, whichh use graphical representations of data and information within the analytics tab.
